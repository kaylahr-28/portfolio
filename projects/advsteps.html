Adventurous Steps

November 2024

  • Figma

This Figma prototype is meant to represent a mobile app designed to track daily movement while also implementing an interactive game experience. Completed in IGME 110: Introduction to Interactive Media.

The Process

We began this group project by brainstorming different interactive experiences. We decided on the concept of gamified fitness. Through research on the gamified fitness market and its users, we found that what's lacking is an app that is accessible (i.e. doesn't solely track steps) and not location-based. So, we created the concept of a gamified fitness app that can track steps and distance travelled, while also not requiring users to physically move to a certain area on a real life map to engage in gameplay.

I took the lead on designing the main wireframe of our prototype. I determined a color palette and font styles that matched our themes of adventure and play. I created the basis of interactions between and within our prototype pages, such as navigation, pop-ups, and the progress bar animation. Throughout the creation of this project, I gained firsthand experience with designing a marketable product, sculpting user interaction, and bringing an interactive experience to life.
